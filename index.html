About The Quick Thrills

The Quick Thrills are a rock band formed in Northern Ontario, Canada. Their sound blends gritty guitars, dark atmosphere, and stripped-back intensity. Inspired by desert rock, blues rock, and modern alternative, The Quick Thrills are currently recording and performing across Ontario and the Midwest.
