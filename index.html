About the Band

Quick Thrills is built on grit, volume, and that worn-in feeling of rock and roll that never really goes out of style. The sound leans vintage without feeling stuck in the past — sharp riffs, driving rhythm, and a live energy that feels immediate.

This isn’t meant to feel polished or corporate. It’s meant to feel real. Like a basement show flyer, an old venue wall covered in posters, or a record sleeve that looks better because it’s a little rough around the edges.
