About

Band Bio

The Quick Thrills are an alternative rock band formed in Northern Ontario, Canada. Their sound blends raw guitars, desert-night mood, stripped-back tension, and a sharp modern edge. Built for dark stages, loud speakers, and long drives after midnight, the band leans into a style that feels restless, cinematic, and real.
